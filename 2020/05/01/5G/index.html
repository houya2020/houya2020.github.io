5G

Posted on 2020-05-01

5G Is Coming This Year.Here’s What You Need to Know

In 2019,a big technology shift will finally begin.It’s a once-in-a-decade upgrade to our wireless systems that will start reaching mobile phone users in a matter of months.

2019年，一场重大的技术变革终于要拉开帷幕了。这是我们的无线系统十年一次的升级，将在几个月内触及移动端手机用户。

But this is not just about faster smartphones. The transition to new fifth-generation cellular networks-known as 5G for short -will also affect many other kinds of devices,including industrial robots,security cameras,drones and cars that send traffic data to one another.This new era will leap ahead of current wireless technology,known as 4G,by offering mobile internet speeds that will let people download entire movies within seconds and most likely bring big changes to vedio games,sports and shopping.

但这不仅仅是让智能手机变得更快那么简单。向新的第五代蜂窝网络的过渡，还将影响许多其它类型的设备，包括工业机器人、监控摄像头、无人机和相互发送交通数据的汽车。这个新时代将实现对于当前无线技术的一次飞跃，5G时代的移动互联网速度能让人们在几秒钟之内便可以下载整部电影，并且很有可能给电子游戏、体育和购物带来巨大变化。

Officials in the United States and China see 5G networks as a competitive edge.The faster networks could help spread the use of artificial intelligence and other cutting-edge technologies.

中美两国的官员们都将5G网络视为一种竞争优势，更快的网络有利于推广人工智能和其他前端科技的使用。

Expect to hear more about 5G soon at events like the big consumer electronics trade show CES in January in Las Vegas and MWC Bracelona(formerly the Mobile World Congress) in Febuary in Spain.Wireless service providers including AT&T and Verizon are already talking up 5G.And device makers are proviewing gadgets that will work with the technology.

拉斯维加斯将于明年1月举办大型电子消费制造商协会，而世界移动通信大会也将于2月份在西班牙巴塞罗那举行，在这样的活动中，预计我们很快就能了解更多关于5G的信息。包括AT&T和Verizon在内的无线服务提供商已经对着5G高谈阔论。而设备制造商们都在预测能与5G技术配套的设备。

Samsung recently demonstrated prototypes of 5G smartphones that are expected to operate on both Verizon and AT&T networks.Many other manufacturers are racing to follow suit,though Apple is not expected in the initial 5G wave.Analysts predict that iPhones with the new technology won’t arrive until 2020.An Apple spokeswomen declined to comment.

三星最近展示了预计会在威瑞森和AT&T网络上运行的5G智能手机样机。许多其他制造商正在竞相跟进，尽管苹果预计不会出现在最初的5G浪潮中。分析人士预测知道2020年苹果才会使用这一新技术。苹果发言人拒绝置评。

Here’s what you need to know.

What exactly is 5G?

Strictly speaking,5G is a set of technical ground rules that define the workings of a cellular network,incluidng the radio frequencies used and how various components like computer chip and antennas handle radio signals and exchange data.

严格来说，5G是一套技术基础准则，它定义了蜂窝网络的工作方式，包括无线电使用频率以及电脑芯片和天线等各种组件如何处理无线电信号和交换数据。

Since the first cellphones were demonstrated in the 1970s,engineers from multiple companies have convened to agree on new sets of specifications for cellular networks,which are designated a new technology generation every decade or so.To get the benefits of 5G,users will have to buy new phones,while carriers will need to install new transmission equipment to offer the faster service.

自20世纪70年代手机首次出现在大众视野以来，多家公司的工程师就已经聚集起来，商讨蜂窝网络各套新规范并达成一致，规定每十年左右发布新一代蜂窝网络技术。为享受5G的产品功效，用户将不得不购买新的手机，而运营商将需要安装新的传输设备以提供更便捷的服务。
